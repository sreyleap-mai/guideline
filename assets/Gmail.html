Set Up Google Gmail

រៀនតម្លើង Gmail ជាមួយការណែនាំជាជំហាន

Follow our guide to create and configure your Gmail account.

Setup Steps

1. Visit this Gmail sign-up page in any browser, such as Microsoft Edge.

2. Click the link in the first step. Click No thanks if creating an account not for a child.

3. Click Yes, continue if creating an account for a child.

4. Complete your first name and last name, then click Next.

5. Enter your birthday and gender, then click Next.

6. Choose a Gmail address or create your own, then click Next.

7. Create a password for your Gmail account, then click Next.

8. Input the parent gmail account, then click Next.

9. Click to agree with the policy, then click I agree.

10. Your Gmail account setup is complete.
