Installation Steps

1. Visit this Thunderbird download page in any browser, such as Microsoft Edge.

2. Click the button. The Thunderbird Installer will automatically offer you the best available version for your computer.

3. Click Open file in the Downloads notification on Microsoft Edge to start the process.

4. Click Choose Standard and click Next.

5. Then click Install this program.

6. Click Finish.

7. When in Thunderbird, set up your existing email address with your username, email, and password.

8. Click the Done button.

9. Then click the Set Default button. Your installation and setup are finished.
